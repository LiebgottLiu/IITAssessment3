  • Group REF

    Our group has decided to divide this assignment into different parts and each of our group members are all assigned to one of these parts of work. So, we are all work on different parts individually and combine all our work at the last day. Our group has started the assignment a bit late, but soon we start discussing the assignment details through the meeting function in the Microsoft Team and assign one job to each member. One of the biggest challenge that our group faced is that the coronavirus pandemic was confirmed to have reached to Australia. It had a huge impact to our working environment. Our school has been closed for months due to the coronavirus so we can not go to school and seek help from our tutor face to face and also, we can’t discuss our work face to face and we can only use the meeting function in Microsoft Team or by text message to communicate with each other.

    This has increased the difficulties of interact with our group members and make it harder to work on the assignment. Another big challenge that our group faced is that one of our group member has suddenly dropped this course and this messed up our plan. Our group has started the assignment late, this experience taught us that we should start working on the assignment early to make sure that we have enough time to work on the assignment and we should improve the interaction time between team members. And by working on this assignment, our group members learnt the importance of team work. While our group are working on this assignment, we faced a lot of challenges and problems. When our group members are struggling on the task, they would ask questions in the group chat and others will try to answer the questions and solve the problem together, this experience taught us that the collaboration skills with team members and team work are very important when doing group.
