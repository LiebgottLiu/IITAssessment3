Our main aim is to “be able to assist students with their homework/assignment questions”. This suits to be our projects aim as it is the overall goal that is hoped to be achieved through the development of this application. Additionally, this is a specific and single aim as it outlines the main and specific objective that is to be accomplished by the app.

  • Goal 1: The incorporation of math teachers into the chatbot is a goal that is needed to be achieved in order to the overall aim of the project. With the incorporation of these specific subject teachers the students will be able to ask their maths related questions thus furthering us to achieving our goal.

  • Goal 2: The incorporation of English teachers into the chatbot is a goal that is needed to be achieved in order to the overall aim of the project. With the incorporation of these specific subject teachers the students will be able to ask their English related questions thus furthering us to achieving our goal.

  • Goal 3: The incorporation of science teachers into the chatbot is a goal that is needed to be achieved in order to the overall aim of the project. With the incorporation of these specific subject teachers the students will be able to ask their science related questions thus furthering us to achieving our goal.

  • Goal 4: By allowing students to pick which subject they need assistance for it will further progress us towards achieving our main goal. This will enable the students to pick which subject they need assistance helping to reach the main goal of the project.
