This is Project group 33

With the development of social telling, more and more new things are being developed and applied in our daily life, which also means that more homework pressure is placed on students. At the same time, parents also need to bear a lot of life pressure, which makes them do not have enough time to accompany their children. So our project is to develop an app or robot to help these parents who have no way to accompany their children. This robot will enter some basic knowledge in advance to answer the students' questions. And some other features

HonSon

TimeFrame

We will get 10 weeks to complete this project that benefits people. After discussing with my team members, we decided to divide these ten weeks into several parts to complete gradually

Step 1: Communicate with customers to determine what functions we need to achieve

We will spend a whole week in constant communication with users, customers and others to ensure that we understand what kind of functions need to be implemented and what kind of requests they put forward. And make the most basic R & D plan
Week 1

Step 2 : Conduct pre-research on the code.

When we have fully understood what kind of functions this program needs to be implemented, we can start to study the code in advance. This is a very effective means to help programmers quickly plan their job requirements
Week 2

Step 3: Coding

After programmers have cleared what they are required to do, we will enter and exit the programming phase. At this stage, all workers will strive to implement their work requirements in code.
Week 3.4

Step 4 : User test

After the programmers have completed the most basic code, we can start the User test phase. At this stage, we need to go to the street to find relevant users to test our program and continuously collect opinions. For the next stage
Week 5

Step 5: improve

Based on user feedback, we will understand which parts we have made good progress. What parts we still need to improve. Next, we will improve as much as possible based on these feedbacks.
Week 6.7

Step 6 : Closedness test

After we have done our best to improve our procedures, we will have to conduct a closure test. Let's test the actual performance when our program is launched. And we need to continuously collect data for further improvement
Week 8

Step 7: improve

After the closed test, we will get more data. After re-aggregating and collating these data, we will reap more suggestions for improvement. After that we will make final adjustments to prepare for the full announcement
Week 9

Step 8 : Announcement procedure

After the final debugging, our program is ready to be released
Week 10
