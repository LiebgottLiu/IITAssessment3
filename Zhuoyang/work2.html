Group Meeting

Communication will be done through Microsoft teams. Team meetings will be done on Microsoft Teams and be recorded however informal conversations will not be recorded and will be discussed within the chat. Meetings are to be done every Monday and Friday weekly. The meetings on Friday will be recorded and formal discussions about the assignment while the Monday meetings will be more informal while being about the assignment. If a group member does not respond we will try to contact them as much as we can. However if they do not respond after many attempts to contact them the group leader will delegate the missing persons responsibilities to other members and the missing group member will receive a negative peer review.
